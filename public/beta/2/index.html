Why are these homes better?

Traditionally, home space has been valued in the number of square feet.
We'd rather maximize designed space in functionality:

  • Typically less than 900 sq/ft (footprint: 13' x 28')
  • Fixed components like bookcases, swings and love seats
  • Very little movable furniture
  • Better use of cubic space
  • Little is better, efficiently meeting a major customer need
  • 3D CAD modeled to precision (see how with Alibre!)
